1.0.0 Version:
The app can calculate MW, Rotatable Bonds, Synthetic Accessibility and H-Bond Donors and Acceptors
The app can showcase some of the main potential functional groups of the molecule, including alcohols, ketones, amides, aldehydes, carb acids, esters, ethers, nitriles, halogens and phenols
The app has the option to add a second molecule to calculate the similarity between the original molecule
